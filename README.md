Introduction
Artificial Intelligence systems such as chatbots and language models generate responses based on prompts provided by users. A prompt is an instruction, question, or statement given to an AI system to obtain a desired response. The quality and clarity of prompts directly influence the accuracy and usefulness of generated outputs.
Prompt engineering is the process of designing effective prompts to improve AI responses. Different prompting methods help users communicate requirements clearly and achieve better outcomes.
Prompting techniques are widely used in:
•	Education 
•	Programming 
•	Data analysis 
•	Research 
•	Business communication 
•	Customer support 
•	Content generation 
•	Healthcare 
•	Finance 
•	Marketing 
Understanding different prompt types helps users interact effectively with AI systems.

1. Straightforward Prompting
Definition
Straightforward prompting means asking direct questions or giving simple instructions without additional formatting.
These prompts are easy to understand and generate immediate responses.
Characteristics
•	Simple 
•	Direct 
•	Clear 
•	Easy to create 
•	Used for factual information
